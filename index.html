— 03 / Questions

The fine print, in plain words.

Is it really free to start?

Yes. Posting work, getting discovered, receiving offers, and getting paid your first $100 — all free. The flat $20/month only kicks in once you've earned $100 on DCC.

How do clients "bid" for me?

Your profile leads with real demos. Clients who like your work send an offer with a budget and brief. You accept the one that's right for you — you're not racing to underbid anyone.

How do payments work?

Through secure escrow. The client funds the project up front; the money is held and released to you when you deliver. No chasing invoices.

Why $20 flat instead of a percentage?

Because a percentage punishes you for doing well. A flat fee means a contractor earning $5,000/month pays the same $20 as one earning $200 — fair, predictable, yours to keep.

Who's building this?

Quantum Melanin Media — creators who got tired of paying to work and decided to build the alternative.
