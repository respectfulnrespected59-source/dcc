## The problem
Freelance platforms charge you to *apply* for work — bids, "Connects," paid per submission, win or lose. You're not the customer there. You're the inventory. Talent is gatekept; the platform eats whether you do or not.

## The flip (DCC)
1. **Show work free** — no bids, no Connects, no fee to apply.
2. **Clients bid for YOU** — demand competes for talent, not the other way around.
3. **$0 up front** — nothing until you've made money here.
4. **$20/month flat, only after you've earned $100** — we make money *after* you do. No percentage skim.
